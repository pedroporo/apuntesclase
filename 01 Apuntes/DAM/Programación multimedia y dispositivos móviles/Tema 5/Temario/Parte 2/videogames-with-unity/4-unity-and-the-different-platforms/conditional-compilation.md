# Conditional Compilation

Source: https://ecm-pmdm-unity.gitbook.io/videogames-with-unity/4.-unity-and-the-different-platforms/conditional-compilation

**Conditional** **compilation** in Unity allows developers to **include** or **exclude** **portions** **of** **code** depending on **target** **platform** or **custom** **symbols**, enabling platform-specific optimizations and features with preprocessor directives like `#if UNITY_IOS`, `#if UNITY_ANDROID`, and more.
