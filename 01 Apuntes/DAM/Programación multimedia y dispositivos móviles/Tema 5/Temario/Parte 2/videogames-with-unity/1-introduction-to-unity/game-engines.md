## What is a Game Engine?

When it comes to gaming, we encounter a multitude of platforms. This diversity implies a wide array of development environments. However, mastering all the native development environments for various devices is a huge challengeâ€”only achievable by large companies with big development teams.

**Game** **engines** are environments that provide developers with comfortable tools to develop their games. These engines often facilitate the generation of apps and games across various platforms, thereby minimizing development costs and effort.
